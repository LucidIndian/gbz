More about this project

What is it?

It'll be like an "Old Farmers' Almanac", but personalized for your garden!

It's going be a web app written in Ruby on Rails that allows you to do a few things for your yard/garden, like:

  • Quickly find a pallete of plants based on your preferences and area
  • CRUD (Create/Read/Update/Delete) your garden over time, tracking yields, growthm or just the pretty pictures you capture each season!

Who's it for?

People that want a killer garden and to make wiser plant choices.

How will people use it?

To create your ZIP code garden, you will...

  1. Enter your email and your garden's ZIP Code
  2. Create your garden by adding info and plants
  3. Get smart plant recommendations based on your location and preferences.
  4. Enable notifications of key events like harvest times, weather warnings, etc.

Why is it being built?

To save the world! I knew just enough to see how tech could help us become better gardeners, and better gardening could save the world, really! In particular, gardening is about selecting plants that are already antifragile in our areas (ZIP codes).
