Thuja plicata

Description

Thuja plicata, or "Western cedar", is a very large an evergreen shrub.

Native to the Pacific Northwest in the US.

Growing conditions

  • Cold hardiness: Zone 2a to 9a
  • Light: Full sun
  • Drought tolerance: High
  • Humidity: 40-80%

Planting tips

  1. Select a location that matches the gorwing conditions
  2. Dig a hole about twice as wide as the root ball
  3. Fill the hole with water and allow it to drain
  4. Untangle the roots
  5. Place plant in the hole and fill in soil around the roots
  6. leave the top of the rootball unburied
  7. Water again and fertilize/mulch as needed
